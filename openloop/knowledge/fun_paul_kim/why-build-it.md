Messaging isn’t just about what you want to say (with a product) - It’s about what your buyers need to hear. 

To craft a compelling message think about these 4 steps:
1) Buyer context - Who they (users) are and what they’re dealing with.
2) Core friction - What keeps breaking, and why they should act. Why does their current situation suck? Break down what’s at stake, and what the cost of inaction is. This is what makes your message feel relevant—like you're reading their mind.
3) Strategic unlock - The transformation your product enables. This is the bridge between their pain and your solution, from what you fix to what they gain.
4) Why you - Why are you the best option to make that shift happen?. The goal isn’t to say you’re the best.
It’s to prove you’re the obvious choice for them.
