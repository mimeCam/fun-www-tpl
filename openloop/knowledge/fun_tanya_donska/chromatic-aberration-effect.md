`Chromatic Aberration` on purpose:

- It can make output designs feel "moody".
- Some designers like to incorporates color fringing to convey motion, duality and slight edge. 
- This effect evokes emotion or can creates an certain atmosphere.
- `Chromatic Aberration` can also draw Focus or Disrupt it.
